Hello! This is a Js callback practice.

Look at the console.

Available Functions:

  • add(num1, num2); - Adds two numbers.
  • subs(num1, num2); - Substracts two numbers.
  • mult(num1, num2); - Multiply two numbers.
  • div(num1, num2); - Divide two numbers.
  • calc(num1, num2, callback) - Apply the callback to the first arguments.
  • runcode(callback) - Executes the function in the argument with 5 seconds of delay.
